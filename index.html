How It Works

Four steps.
One honest number.

01
Tell us your stores
Enter your zip code. WhollyWare maps the retailers within your radius — including warehouse clubs if you have a membership.
02

Allergies, dietary restrictions, brand preferences. Health constraints are hard rules — never traded for a lower price.
03

Weekly sale prices pulled for every tracked category. Unit price math done for you — no mental gymnastics in the aisle.

Gas cost is subtracted. If driving to Costco costs more than you'd save this week, we tell you. Gross savings is not the number that matters.
