How It Works

Three steps to
household savings.

01

Tell us your stores

Enter your zip code. WhollyWare finds the Walmart, Target, Costco, Kroger, and Dollar General locations actually reachable from your address.

02

We pull the weekly circulars

Every week, WhollyWare ingests store sale circulars and compares unit prices across brands, sizes, and stores — automatically normalized.

03

Apply your household filters

Fragrance-free? Dye-free? Allergy constraints? Your household profile filters first — anything that fails your rules never appears, at any price.

04

See your Found Money

Your personalized staple list, organized by store, with net savings shown after gas costs. The number in your pocket — not the flattering number.

The Sincere Strategy®

No brand can pay to appear
in a WhollyWare recommendation.
Not one. Ever.

Every major household staple recommendation platform — coupon apps, store loyalty programs, cashback services — is funded by the retailers and brands they claim to help you compare. Their incentive is to drive you toward higher-margin products. Ours is to find you the actual best price.

🚫

Zero paid placements. Ever.

No brand can pay to appear in a WhollyWare comparison, recommendation, or alert — at any price. This is a founding operating constraint, not a policy we revisit.

⛽

Honest trip costs.

We show net savings after gas. We flag stores where savings don't cover the drive. Don't burn gas to save pennies — we say so when it happens.

🛡️

Health filters are hard rules.

Fragrance-free, dye-free, allergy constraints — these are absolute. They run before the price optimizer. No saving is worth a household health compromise.

🔒

Your data is yours.

We know your household health constraints and shopping habits. That data is never sold, shared, or used for targeting. Period.
